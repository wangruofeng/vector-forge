Vector Forge — Free Online SVG Editor in Your Browser

Vector Forge is a free, browser-based SVG editor. Drop in any SVG to inspect its layers, fine-tune fills, strokes and transforms in real time, and export clean, optimized markup. No install, no account — everything runs locally in your browser.

  • Import SVG by drag-and-drop or file picker
  • Layer tree: select, rename, reorder, show or hide elements
  • Add shapes — rect, circle, ellipse, line, polyline, polygon, path and text
  • Live property panel: fill, stroke, opacity, stroke width, corner radius
  • Inline text editing directly on the canvas
  • Pan and zoom with trackpad pinch, mouse scroll or on-screen buttons
  • Multi-select with per-layer and combined bounding boxes
  • Source view with syntax highlighting and one-click format
  • Full undo / redo history
  • Export optimized, minified SVG
